Protocol 01

Privacy Protocol

1. Data Sovereignty

All data processed through ALTHR systems remains the exclusive property of the client. We do not sell, lease, or share granular business intelligence with third-party AI aggregators.

2. Collection Objectives

We collect operational telemetry solely for the purpose of optimizing system performance and ensuring protocol compliance. This includes API response times, workflow latency, and error logs.

3. Sovereign Encryption

All data at rest is encrypted using AES-256 protocols. Transit data is secured via TLS 1.3. Local AI fine-tuning remains within the client's firewall.

4. Sentiment & Market Intelligence

We process public domain market news and sentiment data to provide competitive advantages. This data is merged with client operational telemetry to create predictive scaling models. Our systems can, for example, monitor global gold prices to automatically adjust jewelry inventory pricing pipelines in real-time, ensuring maximum capital efficiency.

Protocol 02

Operational Terms

1. System Access

Access to ALTHR Agency Systems is granted on a per-seat or per-workflow basis as defined in the master service agreement. Unauthorized reverse-engineering of logic engines is strictly prohibited.

2. Performance Liability

ALTHR is responsible for the integrity of the automation architecture. Client-side errors in third-party API configurations are the responsibility of the client, although ALTHR provides diagnostic support.

3. Termination Protocols

Standard service cycles are 12 months. Early termination requires 90-day notice during which offboarding sequences will preserve data integrity.
